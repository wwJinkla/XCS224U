Natural Language Understanding Cohort 1

Assignment 3 Word-level Entailment with Neural Networks

HW due Sunday, March 22 at 11:59pm PT
Bake-off due Sunday, March 29 at 11:59pm PT

Overview

Assignment 3 and Bake-off Notebook

The general problem is word-level natural language inference.

Training examples are pairs of words (wL,wR),y(wL,wR),y with y=1y=1 if wLwL entails wRwR, otherwise 00

The homework questions ask you to define baseline models for this and develop your own system for entry in the bake-off, which will take place on a held-out test-set distributed at the start of the bake-off. (Thus, all the data you have available for development is available for training your final system before the bake-off begins.)

Supplemental Materials

  • Modeling Semantic Containment and Exclusion in Natural Language Inference (MacCartney and Manning 2008)
  • Learning Distributed Word Representations for Natural Logic Reasoning (Bowman et al. 2015a)
  • The Stanford Natural Language Inference (SNLI) Corpus (Bowman et al. 2015b)
  • Reasoning about Entailment with Neural Attention (Rocktäschel et al. 2015)
